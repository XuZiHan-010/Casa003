Population

The population of Los Angeles county has shown consistent growth from 1990 to 2020, increasing from around 3.5 million to just under 4 million.

The 1990-2000 decade shows higher growth rates across all three locations compared to later decades. However, growth in Los Angeles remains relatively robust compared to national trends, suggesting sustained urban attraction or local growth dynamics

Air Quality

Trends in Moderate Air Quality Days (1980-2020)

This line graph illustrates the annual count of days classified as 'Moderate' in terms of air quality from 1980 to 2020. The data provides insight into the fluctuations and trends in air quality over four decades.

Starting from 1980, the graph shows a steady increase in the number of moderate air quality days, peaking in the year 2000

The peak in 2000, followed by a sharp decline, suggests significant improvements in air quality management or changes in regional industrial activities.

Post-2010, the numbers show a slight recovery but still remain below the year 2000 levels, indicating sustained efforts towards air quality improvement or possibly changes in the factors contributing to air pollution.
